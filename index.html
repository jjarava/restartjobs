ReStart Jobs
  • A job-search platform similar to InfoJobs, created to help immigrants access employment and training opportunities.
  • It allows users to build simple CVs, apply for inclusive job offers, and enrol in language and skills-based courses.
  • The platform also provides basic legal guidance to support documentation processes and social integration.
  • Overall goal: to improve access to formal employment, reduce social exclusion, and foster successful integration into the host society.

How does ReStart Jobs work?

Step 1
Simple registration

Users create an account with basic information, choose their language and indicate their situation (newly arrived, actively looking for work, etc.).

Step 2
Guided CV builder

A step-by-step assistant helps them build a clear CV, even if their experience is informal or their qualifications come from another country.

Step 3
Inclusive job offers

Partner companies committed to diversity post vacancies with simple language and realistic requirements.

Step 4
Ongoing support

The platform suggests language courses, digital skills training and basic legal resources to support full social and labour integration.

Customer Support Specialist

Sun Electronics ¬∑ Madrid ¬∑ Full-time ¬∑ Inclusive employer

Job Description

Provide front-line support to customers by phone, email and chat. Help users understand products, solve basic technical issues and follow up on open cases. Work closely with the technical and sales teams to make sure every customer feels heard and supported.

Desired Profile

  • At least 2 years of experience in a customer support role.
  • Clear and patient communication skills in Spanish.
  • Basic English is a plus, but not mandatory.
  • Empathy, active listening and problem-solving attitude.
  • Open to continuous training and feedback.

How We Support Migrants

Sun Electronics partners with ReStart Jobs to make the hiring process easier for migrants and newcomers. We offer:

  • Help understanding the contract and basic labour rights.
  • Support with documentation needed for hiring.
  • Access to language classes during the first year.
  • A buddy inside the company to guide you in your first months.

Expected social impact

Even though this is a school prototype, the idea behind ReStart Jobs is to show a realistic solution to:

  • Reduce unemployment among migrants and newcomers.
  • Make labour rights and procedures easier to understand.
  • Offer personalised training paths based on each profile.
  • Connect inclusive companies with diverse talent.
